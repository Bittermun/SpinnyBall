Higher fault rates (>10^-2 /hr) or reduced containment thresholds should be tested to locate the actual cascade onset.

Note: k_eff values (4500–9000 N/m) in these MC runs reflect anchor profile constraints, not the analytical $u^2$ scaling. Analytical results in Section 7 show the physical stiffness increases to the Mega-N/m range at 15 km/s.

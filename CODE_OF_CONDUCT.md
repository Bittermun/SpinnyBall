# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in our project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

We expect all contributors to follow these standards:

### Be Respectful

- Treat others with respect and consideration
- Value different viewpoints and experiences
- Give and gracefully accept constructive feedback
- Accept responsibility and apologize to those affected by our mistakes

### Be Inclusive

- Welcome newcomers and help them learn
- Use inclusive language
- Respect different communication styles
- Focus on what is best for the community

### Be Collaborative

- Work together toward shared goals
- Build on each other's ideas
- Ask for help when needed
- Help others when you can

### Be Professional

- Keep discussions focused and productive
- Avoid personal attacks
- Respect privacy and confidentiality
- Follow project guidelines

## Responsibilities

### Project Maintainers

Project maintainers are responsible for:

- Clarifying standards of acceptable behavior
- Responding to reports of unacceptable behavior
- Taking appropriate action when standards are violated
- Ensuring a safe and inclusive environment

### Contributors

Contributors are responsible for:

- Following this code of conduct
- Reporting violations they witness
- Cooperating with maintainers to resolve issues
- Helping maintain a positive community

## Reporting Incidents

### How to Report

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it by:

- Emailing: bittermun@example.com
- Opening a private issue with the "conduct" label

### What to Include

- Description of the incident
- People involved
- When and where it happened
- Any evidence (screenshots, logs, etc.)

### Response Process

1. We will acknowledge receipt within 48 hours
2. We will investigate the report
3. We will determine appropriate action
4. We will communicate outcome to reporter
5. We will take steps to prevent recurrence

## Enforcement

### Consequences

Unacceptable behavior may result in:

- Warning to stop behavior
- Temporary or permanent ban from project
- Removal of contributions
- Reporting to platform administrators

### Appeal Process

If you believe enforcement was unfair:

- Submit an appeal to bittermun@example.com
- Include why you believe the action was unfair
- We will review and respond within 7 days

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to:

- All project spaces (issues, pull requests, discussions)
- Public and private communications
- Project events and meetings
- Any other project-related activities

## Contact

For questions about this code of conduct, contact: bittermun@example.com
